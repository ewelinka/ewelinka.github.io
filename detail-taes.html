TAEs

"Tecnología en Artes Escénicas" - academic course on technology in performing arts.

TAEs was an academic course performed during the second semester of 2015 at the The Faculty of Engineering in Montevideo. It aimed to introduce cross-disiplinary practices at the Engineering Faculty and promote technology-augmented creation in performing arts. Computer science studentes and local dancers devoloped in conjunction short performance pieces that were presented at Centro de Exposiciones SUBTE at the end of the course.

We also orginizaed workshops in 4 different locations in Uruguay.
