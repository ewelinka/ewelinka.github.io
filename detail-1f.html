1/f

Interactive performance with live music and visuals.

1/f was presented in the event Ingeniería de Muestra in October 2015. Interactive projections were a part of audio visual show prepared in collaboration with musicians Christian Clark, Guillermo Berta, Marco Colasso, VJ Juan Pablo Colasso and dancer Ximena Castillo.

Thematic statement

This piece wanted to recreate the unique and fleeting atmosphere of the thunderstorm. The piece was divided into two parts. The first was abstract, based on computer graphics, black and white interactive dance performance. The second part included real nature videos, VJ performance and live music.
