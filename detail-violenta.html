Violenta Ternura

Dance and real-time projections.

Violenta ternura is a dance piece presented in Teatro Solis (Sala Zavala Muniz) of Montevideo in March 2014. This piece was developed in collaboration with the dancer and choreographer Lía Spatakis. The full register of the piece can be found on Vimeo.

Thematic statement

This choreographic composition wanted to explore the social hypocrisy of double standards, the difficulties of loving another, sometimes renouncing self and investigate the limits of love, tolerance and risk. It was designed as a dialogic narrative of broken fragments of parallel stories of love, madness and betrayal.
